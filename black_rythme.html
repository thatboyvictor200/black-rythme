About Lil Black

Victor Eme, known as Lil Black, from Ohafia LGA, Abia State, Nigeria, started his journey immersed in traditional beats. From local stages to wider acclaim, his passion for rap has been unstoppable. Lil Black fuses traditional Nigerian rhythms with modern sounds, crafting songs that reflect his life, dreams, and struggles. His powerful lyrics and high-energy performances have made waves in the industry. Known for his captivating stage presence and storytelling, Lil Black is redefining rap and promises a bright future in the global music scene.
